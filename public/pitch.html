The problem

There's a conversation you keep not having.

🌈 Coming out

to a parent who's never talked about anything this personal.

🚪 Quitting

a job you're loyal to, without getting guilt-tripped into staying.

🫂 A diagnosis

telling your kid you're sick — honest, but still keeping them safe.

💔 Ending it

with someone you still love, without cruelty or false hope.

You rehearse it in the shower — and it falls apart the second they react in a way you didn't script. Practice partners are expensive, awkward, and never play the part convincingly.

The solution

Circles gives you the reps.

Describe the conversation. An agent becomes the other person — defensive, tearful, guilt-tripping, whatever they'll really be — and after every line you say, it tells you exactly where it landed and how to say it better.

🎭 It plays them

An in-character persona whose difficulty adapts to how you're doing — harder when you cope, gentler when you flounder.

👁 It watches you

A separate pass scores your clarity, empathy, and heat — and catches the moment you escalate or freeze.

✅ It decides you're ready

A Readiness score climbs across rounds; the loop self-terminates when you can actually walk in.

Why it fits the theme

The loop is the product.

Not a chatbot with a loop bolted on. Plan → act → observe → self-correct runs every round, and it's on screen the whole time.

Trust · sponsors

The most private data, handled right.

A coming-out, a divorce, a diagnosis. So privacy is built in, not bolted on — and the sponsor tools do real work.

🔒 Private by construction

The demo engine runs fully on-device. Real calls are PII-redacted; high-risk topics can't leave without explicit consent. Conversations are never stored.

🛡 Pomerium

Identity-aware, policy-gated egress at the runtime boundary — mirrored by the app's evaluateEgress().

☁ AWS Bedrock

Claude on Bedrock via a SigV4 signing proxy — a first-class provider, creds never in the browser.

⚡ Akash

Open, low-cost compute for the private inference proxy — no platform lock-in.
